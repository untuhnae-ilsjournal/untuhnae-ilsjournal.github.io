    About The Author

    Anthony A. Tamayo II is a 17-year-old STEM student at STI College Tagum. He enjoys reading, creating, and discovering new things. Curiosity and perfection are his muses, and he has fallen deeply in love with knowledge. Anthony appears to be a boring, lame, and cringy guy, but appearances can be deceiving. He's like a dormant volcano that erupts every now and then. He frequently finds himself at a loss for words or actions, but he always manages to pull it off. In some ways, he struggles to get words out of his mouth, but once he does, he is unstoppable. Taylor Swift, Viola Davis, and Lemony Snicket are Anthony's role models. He's still perplexed as to why some people enjoy writing, but he's willing to give it a shot.



    Purpose of the Website

    The author's goal for the website is to document the journey of his Group's iLS Project in his own unique way. Music has always been a source of comfort for the blogger since he was a child, and he hopes to incorporate it into this project as it progresses. Anthony wishes to convey each chapter of this journey in a musical manner, by including a song that corresponds to the entry that the viewers will construe. Now, just like Taylor Swift's song. Are you "Ready for It"?



    Author's Note: On each chapter entry, a music player can be found in the lower part of the page. Each song that can be played corresponds to the entry and purpose of the chapter. For a more immersive experience, activate the music player by pressing the play button.
