    Chapter Seven: The Real Ending (끝)

    We completed our iLS Projects the following days. We filmed at Energy Park, Apokon Road, for our Physical Education iLS Project. We filmed at Tagum's Gaisano Mall for our Entrepreneurship iLS Project. Gaisano Mall was crowded and quite noisy. Nonetheless, we made due and finished the video. At Robinsons Mall Tagum, we recorded our Inquiries, Investigations, and Immersion iLS Project. We chose it since it was more peaceful than Gaisano Mall. We finished our tasks fast and then took the exams after they were submitted. Following the tests, the iLS Expo commenced. Unfortunately, none of our iLS Projects for Entrepreneurship, Empowerment Technologies, Contemporary Arts, or General Physics were included at the iLS Expo. However, we were chosen as one of the top three candidates in our section for the Work Immersion and Inquiries, Investigations, and Immersion iLS Projects. Despite the fact that we were not recognized, we believe our efforts were rewarding and enjoyable.

    I never imagined I'd be able to complete all of this work in less than two weeks. It was exhausting, but it was also enjoyable and gratifying. However, I would not want to go through this again in college. STI College Tagum is a demanding and nurturing institution. I would never want to return here (just joking). Jokes aside, I've learned a lot during my stay at STI College Tagum. It was difficult and depressing, but I'm still here, battling and living my life.
